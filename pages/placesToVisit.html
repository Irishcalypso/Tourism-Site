Eyre Square

Eyre Square is a wonderful place to visit when going to Galway. The park is within the city centre, adjoining the nearby shopping area of William Street and Shop Street. Galway railway station is adjacent to Eyre Square.

Salt Hill

Salt Hill has a fabulous border walk along the sea! Along with that are various sea side attractions such as and arcade and pool. Try your hand with salthills famous Blackrock diving tower.

Spanish Arch

Spanish Arch was built in 1594, it was built to house soldiers who kept watch and manned cannons on the roof. It is proclaimed as a must see when visiting Galway.

Latin Quarter

If visiting Galway in the summer you will love the Latin Quarter! It is a little place nestled beside the River Corrib. You'll find that throughout the summer there will be various activitys and adventures to be had not to mention the restauruants you can visit.

City Museum

At Galways Museum you can view and experience some of Galways archaeology, sea life and heritage. Find out more at their website.

Atlantaquaria

Atlantaquaria is ireland largest native species aquarium. It is located over seeing Galway Bay along the wild Atlanttic Way! Find out more and book your tickets here!
