Top 5 Places To Stay In Galway

1: The Connact Hotel

The Connacht Hotel tops this list for its stylish and comforatble rooms! It is located just outside the city center a 15 minute walk or 5 minute bus ride.

5 Stars: Excellent and friendly service! Front staff were very helpful and quick to respond to requests via telephone from the room. - Dylan Book Here!

2: The Imperial Hotel

The Imperial Hotel Is right in the heart of Galway "Eyre Square". Just a step outside from any shops and attraction you could want when visiting Galway!

5 Stars: Had an excellent stay here for a long weekend. Staff were more than helpful, friendly and respectful both on the ground floor and elsewhere. Room were a good size, bed comfy and clean. - Brian Book Here!
